Welcome to Signlingu!

At the moment, we're prioritizing working on the BISINDO curriculum, with future possibilities of adding more sign languages, such as International Sign Language, etc.

Our exercise approach is inspired by the Duolingo method, which encourages learners to memorize through repetition.

You may access this platform through a desktop or phone, though it's easier via desktop since you'll be able to use keyboard shortcuts.

Use the numbers (1, 2, 3, 4) shortcuts to select the correct answer.

Click on Enter to continue to the next question.

At the final page, click on the space bar to retry the lesson.

To close the lesson at any moment, click on the Esc button.
